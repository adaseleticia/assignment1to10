Introduction

Yolo is an imaginative name to describe a group of lasies ready to live their life to the best of their experince. their theme being we are our own limit believe they can do every and all things if they put their mind to it. From handling heavy metals, to all control programs in technology these ladies aim to make the world a better place.

2019 Objectives

  1. Reach out to 3 basics schools every month(with target to 6th graders).
  2. Organize training sections for remote commuties once every month (JSS students).
  3. Computer literacy training to adults once every two month.
  4. Providing a computer center to 2 comunities in North Tongu.
